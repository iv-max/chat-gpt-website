Kubernetes Platform

Kubernetes Helm Blue‑Green

Overview

I migrated core services to Kubernetes to improve reliability and scale. I implemented robust deployment strategies and standardized Helm charts for consistent rollouts.

What I Built

  • Dockerized Node/Express and React services
  • Helm charts with config maps, secrets, and HPA
  • Readiness/liveness probes and resource limits
  • Blue‑green & rolling updates with zero‑downtime goals
  • Ingress with TLS and rate‑limit rules

Results

Incidents related to deployments decreased significantly. Horizontal scaling handled 3× traffic spikes without downtime, while costs remained predictable.
