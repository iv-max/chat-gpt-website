Observability Stack

Prometheus Grafana ELK

Overview

To reduce MTTR and improve reliability, I delivered a unified observability platform that correlates metrics, logs, and alerts.

What I Built

  • Prometheus metrics collection and custom exporters
  • Grafana dashboards with SLOs and error budgets
  • ELK stack for centralized logs and search
  • Alerting & on‑call workflows with actionable playbooks
  • Security hardening and RBAC for least‑privilege access

Results

We cut median time to detect issues by ~60% and established clear SLOs, making on‑call calmer and more predictable.
